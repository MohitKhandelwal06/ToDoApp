How To Use

Welcome to the ToDo ticket app, here you can create maintain your day-to-day tasks or list everything that you have to do. You can also prioritise a particular task by giving it a specific color. Here we have provided 4 colors i.e. light pink, light green, light blue,black. In the toolbar provided on the top, you can see these colors,if you click on a particular color all the tickets made with that color will appear on screen, on double clicking on any color all the tickets will again appear on screen.
Next to it is a add sign which is used to create a new ticket, all you have to do i click on it select a color on the basis of the priority of the work write some text and then click on shift button on your keyboard to generate the ticket.
After that is the cross sign which is used to remove a ticket. All you have to do is to click on the cross button and then click on the ticket you want to remove.
You can also change the priority color by clicking on the color strip.
To edit a particular ticket click on lock icon.

Press Okay to continue.
